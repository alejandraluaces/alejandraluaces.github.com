--- layout: project-posts category: projects title: Semantic HTML Table Project date: April 5, 2013 ---

For this project, I worked on replicating something tabular from print or internet. I decided I'd do the NPR Show schedule, as I listen to the station religiously, and the schedule on their website is messed up.

Tables are really no big deal--they're all just about remembering the placement of other object around them. I used html5 tags in this project--thead, tbody, and caption. I also added some phone icons I downloaded from Fontello, and positioned them relative to their starting positions within their compartments. Fortunately, their positions were all pretty similar, so it only took one tag to manipulate them.

I'd never worked with tables beyond a table-based layout (the greatest of faux pas!), but now I feel much more comfortable producing them to work with complicated tabular data.
