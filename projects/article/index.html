Railroads are by far the safest, most efficient, and most environmentally friendly mode of transportation. Why not do a story on 18-wheelers? Studies have shown that if big trucks paid road-use taxes commensurate with the expense of maintaining roads due to truck traffic, much of the freightthat goes by truck would go by rail instead. That cargo could be shipped more safely, more cheaply, and with less environmental impact.
