These are some mockups I did for my IPM today. I am still working on the school registration project. The shot on the left is the control panel, while the shot on the right is the main registration form.

Another fun part of this project was making the logo for "Hillsborough Pigeon Preparory School".. ha. Glad to be able to inject a little bit of what I am best at into an otherwise sometimes tedious & slightly intimidating project.
